How to make the perfect dessert

Half Baked Cookies!!!

Step 1

First, you make chocolate chip cookies, but only bake them for half the time so they stay nice and gooey.

Step 2

Second, you place a cookie or two in a bowl and place vanilla icecream on top, or any icecream of your choosing.

Step 3

and lastly, you add choclate and caramel syrup and top it off with some whipped cream.

You now have the best dessert in the world. Enjoy!!!
